ABOUT US
The Project

“Luminous Wizards” was established in 2016, by a group of professional dancers with the idea of combining high technologies with art.

The project is based on the mixing of different dance styles (Hip-hop, Popping, House, Breaking, Locking) with acrobatic elements.

The Team

Our team team consists on 8 people (7 dancers + technician).

In our team, we do everything we can, from the idea of the show and its choreography to the full development of costumes and props. So if you have an interesting idea and a budget, we are ready to make it a reality!

Our Experience

Over the past 6 years, our show ballet has visited many places in our world. And wherever we worked, we received a lot of positive feedback from both viewers and employers. Currently we have worked in such countries: UAE, Peru, China, Germany, France and Latvia.

But more to come!

We guarantee the quality of our shows and are ready to prove it on the stage.
